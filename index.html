WELCOME TO OUR HOUSE

Riot House is an Elysium for music-festival crazies in Cardano. We explore the intersection of high-quality art, music, and blockchain technology to create an unforgettable experience for everyone in the community. It's a place where you can bring out your individuality and own style without any judgment from the outside world.
We set to build a place where the biggest parties are set to happen in Cardano by offering a multidisciplinary experience to let loose, be creative, and connect with others who share the same passions.
Join us to be updated with more information about the project as we build the Riot House together!

ROADMAP

  • PLANNING

    This was the think tank stage. Where we did extensive research and tried our very best to understand the current landscape… forreals tho, we really did try our best, cause it’s really hard to focus when you’re high and drunk. But eventually, we sobered up, studied existing projects, and interviewed seasoned founders about what they did that made their projects succeed or fail. This stage allowed us to fine-tune our approach to making our vision for Riot House a reality.

  • CREATION

    Delivering high-quality art beyond the industry standard, that’s what we’re all about. So we worked closely with a collective of artists by keeping them in the dungeon and injecting them with colorful liquids in order to achieve our vision of embodying the festival spirit in all of our artwork.

  • COMMUNITY

    NO paid promotion, NO Like farming, and strictly NO grinding… cause we ain’t boomers… and tbh grinding is weird af. So we don't wanna force it, growing Riot House organically is the way. In this space, great art will always resonate with people who have great taste. And that's who we want in our house.

  • MINT

    After nagging every developer in the Cardano space, we eventually ended up with ADA Anvil. Because they’re the only ones who can stand our stubbornness. So they’ll be taking care of the Riot House: The Kick-Off mint. This means if something goes wrong during the mint, you know who to point your pitchforks to. But when everything goes as it should, we’ll gladly take all the credit… jk.

  • ROADMAP V2

    After seeing who’s got our back in our Kick Off Party, we’ll make sure that we’ve got theirs moving forward.
